⚖️ Prevention & Awareness

Report Crimes

Every crime must be reported. Filing an FIR is your right. Police cannot refuse to register an FIR for cognizable offences.

Know Your Rights When Arrested

You have the right to know why you're arrested (Article 22), consult a lawyer, be produced before a magistrate within 24 hours, and not be tortured.

Free Legal Aid

If you can't afford a lawyer, the State must provide one. Call NALSA at 15100 for free legal services.
